## **Updates in 1.3**

### Greetings all! It's been a while since the last -major- update, so do we have some changes in store!
### We have changed how the Bygone generates as whole entirely, reworked the Amber Desert biome, revamped the Ancient Hook, added a new mob and so much more!
### PLEASE keep in mind you WILL have to delete your /bygone/ dimension folder before updating! Some of the changes will have made things unstable in older worlds!
### Please join us in the official [Discord](https://discord.gg/DMW28XH235)!

# New features in 1.3:
* Completely overhauled the Bygones world generation! (See below for more details!)
* Overhauled the look of the Amber Desert biome.
* Overhauled the Ancient Hook.
* Added the Whiskbill and Whiskbill Spawn Egg.
* Added the Amber Sand, Amber Sandstone, Cut Amber Sandstone, Smooth Amber Sandstone and Chiseled Amber Sandstone blocksets.
* Added the Amber Clump.
* Added the Primordial Beach biome.
* Added the Verdigris Scrap Block and Verdigris Block.
* Added the [REDACTED] block, found only in Abandoned Farms.
* Added Glow Gourd Seeds.

* Glow Gourds now have growth stages!
* Big Beaks now have multiple variants! (Including some special ones for Pride Month)!
* Maps now work in the Bygone! They "fill out" depending on what Y level the Players feet are at!

# Terrain Generation changes and additions:
* Added the "Primordial Beach" biome
* Added noise Pillars to the terrain
* Verdigris Scrap Blocks generate near the bedrock layer
* New cave generation
* Redistributed biomes (Beaches will generate next to Oceans, Amber Deserts are less common, and  Primordial Oceans are smaller)
* Made the overall terrain more traversable by flattening it!
* Ores generate more as a whole in order to encourage exploring cave expeditions.

# Amber Desert changes and additions:
* Is now made up of Amber Sand and Amber Sandstone.
* Increased amount of Amber "boulders" in the biome.
* Turned down the light levels of all Amber blocks.
* Patches of Amber Clumps generate on walls and ceilings.
* Pointed Amber now generates only on ceilings.
* Tweaked the fog and sky colors to fit the dimension better!
* Removed Creosotes from generating in the biome.

# Ancient Hook changes:
* Ancient Hook now discards when in liquids. (this is to fix strange behaviour with water),
* Ancient Hook now discards when the player is further than 64 blocks away.,
* Ancient Hook now discards when the shift key is pressed.
* Ancient Hook now attaches to blocks way faster as its shoot speed has been increased a lot.
* Added Ancient Hook as loot in Bygone Mineshafts.
* Ancient Hook sounds are planned for 1.3.1!

# The Whiskbill
* A new passive mob found only in the Underhang.
* Will seek out fully grown Glow Gourds and devour them, spitting out the Seeds!
* Will retreat from nearby players and poison players that hurt them!
* Temporarily are using Sniffer sounds, but unique sounds are planned for 1.3.1!
* Can be bred with Gourd Slices!

## The first update of the new year is here!! We're introducing a brand new, fungal biome -- The Shelfhollows!
You will find them generating alongside the Undergrove biome!

# New features in 1.1.0a:
* Added the Shelfhollow biome.
* Added the Orange/Pink/Purple Mushroom Blocks.
* Added the Orange/Pink/Purple Fungal Brickset.
* Added Shelf Mycelium and Shelf Mold blocks.
* Added new vegetation: Shelf Fungus, Mold, Roots, and Sprouts.
* Added the (Fungal) Parent and Child mobs.
* Added Fungal Parent mob spawn egg.
* Added Claystone Bricks blockset.
* Added stonecutter recipes for the Cobbled Bystone/Byslate family of blocks.

> Shelfhollow Biome
* A quiet land filled with giant Mushroom Shelves and the occasional Sporeshroom.
* Spore particles fly around the player, but breathing them in isn't that bad for you!

> Orange/Pink/Purple blocksets
* Found naturally-occuring on the cliffs of the Shelfhollow Biome!
* Each color has its own bounciness ratio: Orange will slowly stop a players bounces, Pink will almost immediately stop a players bounces, and Purple doubles the players bounces!
* You can craft a Brick blockset from each Mushroom Block as well!
* Both blocksets are preferred tool is Axe.

> Shelf Mycelium and Shelf Mold Blocks
* Both make up the groundscape of the Shelfhollow, coating any Bystone it can find.
* Will naturally spread to any Bystone blocks in its vicinity.
* You can place any Nether Fungi blocks on them as well!

> Shelfhollow Vegetation
* The landscape of the Shelfhollow is literred with new plants and fungi.
* THe Shelf Fungus/Roots/Sprouts can all be found growing from Shelf Mycelium.
* The Shelf Mold will only be found growing out of Shelf Mold Blocks.

> (Fungal) Parent & Child mob
* A new, overly protective mob that will roam the Shelfhollows with their young. Do -not- approach the child if you don't want to be attacked!
* The Parent will poison the player, as well as damaging them normally from its attack.
* The Child will stick close to its Parent, and shy away from the Player.

> Prototaxite Stem
* An experimental new block, does not currently have any uses nor is it available in Survival.
* More coming soon!
